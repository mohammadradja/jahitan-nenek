# 🧵 CONTEXT

## Project Overview
**Jahitan Nenek** is a premium e-commerce platform specializing in handmade knitwear. The platform aims to blend traditional artisanal craftsmanship with a modern, high-end digital shopping experience.

## Vision
To become the leading destination for luxury handmade knitwear, empowering local artisans while providing customers with a warm, "editorial-style" shopping journey.

## Core Values
- **Warmth**: A human-centered design that feels personal and handcrafted.
- **Elegance**: Premium aesthetics with editorial-grade layouts.
- **Heritage**: Celebrating the tradition of knitting in a modern context.
- **Transparency**: Clear communication of production stages and shipping.

## Target Audience
- Fashion enthusiasts looking for unique, artisanal pieces.
- Gift seekers looking for high-quality, handmade items.
- Customers who value sustainability and slow fashion.

## Business Model
- Direct-to-consumer (D2C) sales through the platform.
- Multi-role management system (Superadmin, Admin, Customer).
- Integrated logistics (RajaOngkir) and payments (QRISLY/Midtrans).

## Current Project Focus
- Optimizing the administrative experience (Dashboards).
- Centralizing system configurations (SiteSettings).
- Enhancing visual consistency across all modules.
- Implementing robust reporting and data management.
